Our Studio Philosophy

At Kind of Whimsical Games, we believe in creating experiences that stick with you - games that feel like discovering a hidden treasure in a familiar place. We make games that are accessible to all ages while offering layers of meaning for those who seek them.

Working as a duo allows us to stay nimble, passionate, and true to our vision. Every project is a labor of love, handcrafted with attention to detail and a dash of the unexpected.

Our name says it all - we're just kinda whimsical, balancing playfulness with purpose in everything we create.
